Barky

- Toy Pet
- Realistic behavior
- Fetch, Stunts, activities
- Roaming mode
- Security mode

According to the statistics, many people buy a dog as they want a toy and when they get bored of it they tend to stop taking care of the dog as well as they did before

Barky was created in order to mimic a dog as realistic as possible, maintaining at the same time the look of a toy which you can put away once you are done playing with him.

He can do stunts, fetch, and many other entertaining things that you can find and activate on his menu.

If you're done playing with him, he also has a roaming mode, which will make him move around the house or the yard.

Barky can be also set to security mode where he will guard the house or the yard and will send a video notification on your phone if someone trespasses your property.

Terry

- Evolving Robot
- Toy or Combat Robot
- Battle Mode
- Unable to harm living beings
- 100% hackproof
- Yearly tournament

Terry has been created as an interactive gaming toy pet. Terry is not just your regular robot toy, he starts as a very basic robot toy but you need to help him develop and upgrade.

Once a certain evolution progress has been made we will ship you different upgrades, free of charge.

After a certain ammount of upgrades, battle mode will be unlocked along with a long list of battle customizations and upgrades.

Terry has been programmed in such a way that he can't hurt any beings in any way, and his software and hardware are 100% hackproof due to quantum security

We will host a yearly championship with great rewards for the best warriors but more news regarding this will be published early 2023
