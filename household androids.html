Joya

- Household maintenance
- Sensorial Vision
- Great mobility and accuracy
- 360° Vision
- Cleaning products storage

Joya is our first robot that we have created. She is a humanoid robot, with a friendly design and attitude, she's great with kids and she has advanced household-related tech as following:

- Perfect dishwashing capabilities due to the multiple sensors integrated in her hands which can detect every known harmful bacteria and viruses and will clean each dish to perfection

- EYtron 3.2 vision which allows her to detect even the smallest particles of dust for a perfect vacuuming and furniture cleaning

- Advanced joints and tendons developed for superhuman accuracy

- 360° cameras for great sense of direction

- She has multiple containers in her chest for each type of cleaning products meaning that you do not have to worry about storing cleaning products anymore.

Primo

- Security
- Deployable land sensors
- Small firearms
- Emergency calling
- Holographic emergency training

Primo is your own guardian angel. He has been created for your own good, he is your little security officer, right at home.

We have developed him in such a way that he can detect threat with 100% accuracy.

Primo is equiped with land sensors that he drops when you leave the house or at night (this can be setup) so he can have 100% knowledge of the house surroundings and will alert any trespassers that if they do not leave the property, police will be called

Primo is also equiped with small firearms so he can protect you when you are home and a burglary will happen, alerting the cops at the same time and the ambulance if needed. However he will not use firepower if you are not at home.

Primo can tell the difference between a passed out and a sleeping person and can call a ambulance whenever it is required.

Extra, in case of life-threatening situation, Primo has a hologram that can instruct you how to react in different situations, from performing a CPR to where is the best spot to hide in your house when an earthquake is happening.

Leyla

- Children care and education
- Best pediatric tech & knowledge
- Customizable education
- Violence-free education curriculum

Leyla is the most advanced robot we have ever created. Her area of operability: Customizable Children care and education.

Leyla can raise a child to high standards using the best techniques there are in pediatrics.

The parents can choose what type of education and worldview Leyla will give to their child including religion and politics.

All Leyla's knowledge is non-violent, meaning she does not teach nor practice agression under any form.

We however recommend using Leyla only during workhours as no robot can replace a parent.
