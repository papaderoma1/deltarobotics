Patrick Hugsworth
CEO
Patrick joined Delta Robotics in 2006 having worked in sales roles previously within IT and outside. He has been contributing to the company's growth ever since in a number of increasingly-senior sales positions. In 2006 Patrick joined the Sales Leadership team and alongside his peers in that team is responsible for delivering the company's global sales targets through the creation and execution of strategy, teams and processes.

Zhang Jingguo
COO
Ji was a founding member of a successful UK robotics consulting company and one of the organisations that would ultimately become Delta Robotics. During his career Ji had a series of sales, marketing and business development roles. He has over 20 years experience in the robotics sector focusing on robotics for the Financial Services sector.

Sarvesh Chandra
Team Leader
Sarvesh was Country Manager for Velocity Partners before the company merged with Delta Robotics. With more than 25 years of working in IT and managing global accounts, he is a people’s person at heart, a trait which blended well with his responsibility of overseeing and integrating large, multicultural teams. As of December 2018, Sarvesh is the Team Leader for Delta Robotics
